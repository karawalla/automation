Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I choose a day and time?

A: Place your mouse in an available time slot under the date you prefer, then click to select it. You also can choose a day and time using your Tab key to move to a cell, and Shift+Tab to move back to a previous cell. Hit enter to select that day and time.

Q: How do I see the following week?

A: To the left and right of the calendar are arrows. Select the right arrow to advance to the next week. Use the left arrow to return to the previous week.
