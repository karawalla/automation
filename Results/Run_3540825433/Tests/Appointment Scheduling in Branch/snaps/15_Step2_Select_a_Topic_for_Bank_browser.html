Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I have to be a Bank of America customer?

A: No, anyone can use Bank by Appointment to make an appointment to discuss most types of personal or small business banking needs.

Q: What services can I discuss when I make an appointment using Bank by Appointment?

A: Appointments can be made for many services supported by our personal and specialized services bankers,
such as account openings and account management.

Please note, that although our banking centers offer a full range of services (including teller services),
Bank by Appointment covers only non-teller services handled by our personal and small business bankers.
Additionally, if you have questions regarding Investments and Wealth Management Services,
please learn more about these services on our website or by calling 1.800.926.1111 for Self-Directed Investment
Banking, or 1.800.822.2222 for Full Service Investing.

Q: My topic isn't listed here. Can I still make an appointment?

A: Yes. If your specific need is not listed, please choose the topic that is most similar to your need. You may also use the Comments section to include more detail about your topic. Please be aware that appointments can only be scheduled for personal or small business banking needs. If you have questions regarding Investments and Wealth Management Services, please learn more about these services on our website or by calling 1.800.926.1111 for Self-Directed Investment Banking, or 1.800.822.2222 for Full Service Investing.

Q: When I select my non-English language preference, am I guaranteed to speak to someone, in person, who speaks that language?

A: The banking center will do its best to have an associate onsite who speaks the language you requested. However, if one is not available at that banking center, we will try to provide a translator over the telephone that will assist with the appointment.

Q: What is a small business specialist?

A: Small business specialists have proven expertise in evaluating small business financial needs and matching those needs with Bank of America’s world-class financial products and services. Their aim is to make it easier for small business owners to start and grow their business. They are uniquely trained and positioned to ensure that all of the capabilities and partnerships of Bank of America are readily available to help small business owners meet their goals.

Q: I want to speak with a small business specialist. The banking center I selected doesn't have one. Do I need to choose
a different location?

A: Yes, you will need to choose a banking center that has small business specialist. You can see which ones have small business specialists by looking for the icon in the list of banking centers on Bank by Appointment.
