Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I have to be a Bank of America customer?

A: No, anyone can use Bank by Appointment to make an appointment to discuss most types of personal or small business banking needs.

Q: What is an eRecommend number?

A: If you’re a Small Business owner who’s used our online eRecommend tool to get an account recommendation, you would have received an eRecommend number. The eRecommend number provides a way to access your account recommendation, make edits to your recommendation or apply for accounts. You can also give your eRecommend number to a bank representative so they can access your recommendation.
