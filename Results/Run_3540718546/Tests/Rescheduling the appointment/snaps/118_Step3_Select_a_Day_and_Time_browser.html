Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Information about selecting a day and time for your appointment.How do I choose a day and time?

A: You can use your mouse to select an available appointment on the date you prefer. You also can choose an appointment using your Tab key to move to a cell and Shift+Tab to move back. Select Enter to confirm the day and time.

Q: Information about seeing the calendar for the next week.How do I see the following week?

A: To the left and right of the calendar are arrows. Select the right arrow to advance to the next week. Use the left arrow to return to the previous week.

Q: Information about seeing available appointments for Platinum Privileges Service Center.I don't see a day or time that works for me. Can I call the Platinum Privileges Service Center when it’s convenient?

A: Platinum Privileges customers will reach the Platinum Privileges Service Center by calling 1.800.432.1000 and authenticating in the phone menu when prompted. The team offers extended hours of operation for your convenience 8 a.m.-12 a.m. Monday-Friday and 8 a.m.-8 p.m. Saturday-Sunday (Eastern Time).
