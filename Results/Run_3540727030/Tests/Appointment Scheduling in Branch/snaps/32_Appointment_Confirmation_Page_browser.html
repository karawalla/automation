Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When should I arrive for my appointment?

A: You can arrive for your appointment as scheduled and check-in with the Lobby Leader. In the event that you are running late, we will hold your appointment for at least 10 minutes, depending upon the number of customers waiting to speak with one of our associates.
